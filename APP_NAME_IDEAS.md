# App Name Brainstorming - Official Name Ideas

## Current State
- **Package Name**: habits
- **Display Name**: Habit Tracker
- **Description**: A mobile habit tracker app built with Flutter with Firebase Authentication

## Name Categories & Ideas

### 1. Minimalist & Direct
These names clearly communicate the app's purpose with simplicity.

- **HabitFlow** - Emphasizes the natural flow of building habits
- **HabitStack** - Building habits one on top of another
- **HabitPath** - Your journey to better habits
- **Habitual** - Simple, memorable, says "habits" in the name
- **HabitWise** - Wisdom through consistent habits
- **HabitCore** - Core habit tracking functionality
- **Daily Flow** - Focus on daily routine and flow state
- **StreakKeeper** - Emphasizes the streak tracking feature

**Pros**: Clear purpose, easy to find in app stores, SEO-friendly
**Cons**: May be less unique, harder to trademark

### 2. Playful & Memorable
These names are fun, catchy, and easy to remember.

- **Habitica** (Note: Already exists - avoid)
- **Ritualify** - Making habits into rituals
- **Streakify** - Playful take on building streaks
- **HabbitApp** (with double 'b') - Wordplay on rabbit/habit speed
- **Momentum** - Simple, powerful word about building momentum
- **Nudge** - Gentle push toward better habits
- **Snap Habits** - Quick, snappy habit tracking
- **Tick Daily** - Simple daily check-ins
- **Habit Hero** - Gamification angle
- **Habitue** - French-inspired, means "regular patron"

**Pros**: Memorable, brandable, unique
**Cons**: May not immediately convey purpose

### 3. Professional & Polished
These names sound sophisticated and premium.

- **Cultivate** - Growing better habits
- **Ascent** - Rising through consistent effort
- **Nexus Habits** - Connection point for all your habits
- **Catalyst** - Sparking positive change
- **Pinnacle** - Reaching your peak performance
- **Elevate** - Lifting yourself through habits
- **Thrive** - Flourishing through consistency
- **Propel** - Moving forward with purpose
- **Refine** - Continuously improving yourself
- **Mindful Habits** - Conscious habit building

**Pros**: Premium feel, appeals to productivity enthusiasts
**Cons**: May feel too corporate for some users

### 4. Nature & Growth Inspired
These names use natural metaphors for habit formation.

- **Evergreen** - Habits that last all year
- **Sprout** - Starting small and growing
- **Bloom** - Flourishing through habits
- **Grove** - Collection of habit "trees"
- **Seedling** - Beginning of growth
- **Flourish** - Thriving through consistency
- **Roots** - Building strong foundations
- **Harvest** - Reaping the rewards of consistency

**Pros**: Positive associations, universal appeal
**Cons**: May not immediately suggest "habits"

### 5. Tech-Modern & Clean
These names have a modern, tech-forward feel.

- **Habitude** - Habit + Attitude
- **HabitOS** - Operating system for your habits
- **Routine.io** - Modern domain-style naming
- **Habitas** - Modern twist on "habitats"
- **HabitKit** - Toolkit for habits
- **DailyStack** - Stacking daily habits
- **LoopKit** - Building habit loops
- **ChainLink** - Don't break the chain methodology

**Pros**: Appeals to tech-savvy users, modern feel
**Cons**: May alienate non-technical users

### 6. Short & Sweet (One Word)
These ultra-short names are easy to say and remember.

- **Loop** - Habit loops, very short and memorable
- **Build** - Building better habits
- **Rise** - Rising to your potential
- **Forge** - Forging new habits
- **Track** - Simple and direct
- **Tally** - Keeping count
- **Chain** - Habit chains/streaks
- **Grid** - Visual grid of habits
- **Stride** - Making consistent strides

**Pros**: Extremely memorable, great for branding
**Cons**: Hard to find available domains/trademarks

### 7. Motivational & Aspirational
These names inspire and motivate users.

- **Better Daily** - Becoming better every day
- **GrowDaily** - Daily growth focus
- **Day One** - Every day can be day one
- **Progress Path** - Path to progress
- **WinStreak** - Building winning streaks
- **Victory Loop** - Consistent victories
- **Champion Daily** - Daily wins
- **Goal Grove** - Growing your goals

**Pros**: Motivating, positive energy
**Cons**: May feel too intense for casual users

## Top Recommendations

### Most Brandable & Available
1. **Habitue** - Unique, sophisticated, French-inspired
2. **Momentum** - Powerful one-word name, universal appeal
3. **Cultivate** - Professional yet approachable
4. **Loop** - Simple, memorable, tech-modern
5. **Evergreen** - Positive natural metaphor

### Best for App Store Discoverability
1. **HabitFlow** - Clear purpose, good SEO
2. **Daily Flow** - Searchable, describes function
3. **StreakKeeper** - Highlights key feature
4. **HabitPath** - Clear and descriptive

### Most Unique & Memorable
1. **Habitue** - Uncommon word, memorable
2. **Nudge** - Simple, friendly, different
3. **Sprout** - Visual, growth-oriented
4. **Forge** - Strong, memorable

## Considerations for Final Selection

### Domain Availability
- Check .com, .app, .io availability
- Consider alternatives if primary domain unavailable
- Social media handle consistency (@appname)

### App Store Search
- Search existing apps to avoid confusion
- Consider SEO keywords in name
- Check trademark conflicts

### International Appeal
- Easy to pronounce in multiple languages
- No negative connotations in other languages
- Universal symbolism and meaning

### Branding Potential
- Logo design possibilities
- Visual identity alignment
- Tagline development ("Habitue: Where Habits Take Root")

### Technical Considerations
- Package name change (com.example.habits â†’ com.example.newname)
- Bundle identifier updates
- Firebase project naming
- GitHub repository implications

## Next Steps

1. **Shortlist**: Narrow down to 3-5 favorites
2. **Availability Check**: Domain, app store, trademark
3. **User Testing**: Get feedback from potential users
4. **Visual Mockup**: Test name with logo concepts
5. **Final Decision**: Make the choice and implement

## Current Implementation Impact

If a new name is chosen, the following files need updates:
- `pubspec.yaml` - Package name
- `lib/main.dart` - MaterialApp title
- `android/app/build.gradle` - applicationId and namespace
- `android/app/src/main/AndroidManifest.xml` - android:label
- `web/index.html` - title and apple-mobile-web-app-title
- `web/manifest.json` - name, short_name, description
- `README.md` - All references to "Habit Tracker"
- Documentation files in `docs/`
- `.github/copilot-instructions.md` - Header reference

---

**Recommendation**: Based on uniqueness, brandability, and availability likelihood, I recommend **Habitue** or **Momentum** as the top choices. Both are memorable, professional, and have strong branding potential while being different enough from existing apps in the space.
